Week 8: Mindful Movement
This week, focus on moving your body in a way that feels good—no pressure, just joy.
Why This Matters:
Movement is about celebrating your body, not punishing it.
Mindful movement builds a stronger connection between your body and mind.
Your Practice for the Week:
Experiment with 2–3 types of movement (e.g., walking, stretching, dancing).
Pay attention to how your body feels during and after.
Journal: What kind of movement brings you joy?
This is your chance to move in a way that feels empowering and fun. Let’s celebrate your body!
